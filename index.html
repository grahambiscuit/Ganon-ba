The technology behind the vision

Ganon Ba uses the same stack that powers professional urban planning tools and game studios.

Rendering
Three.js
WebGL-powered 3D engine. Real perspective, dynamic lighting, and street-level camera that runs in any browser.
Map data
OpenStreetMap
Real Philippine street geometry, building footprints, and road classifications — updated by the community daily.
3D Cities
Cesium Ion
Photorealistic 3D tiles of real Metro Manila buildings streamed at full resolution with Google Maps integration.
Data science
PH Open Data
PSA, MMDA, DPWH, and DOTr datasets power every impact metric. Real numbers, not guesses.
